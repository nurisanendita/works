Who am I?

As a recent graduate from Information Technology of Universitas Gadjah Mada, for the last 2 years I'm focusing to learn about UX design and research. I able to conduct the design process based on user-centered design approach to enhance the user experience of web and mobile app products. Beside, I also have capability to develop front-end with JavaScript framework, HTML, and CSS.

I love to design and create a meaningful and usable product. My concern is about deliver products like what user needed, that can solve their problems.
